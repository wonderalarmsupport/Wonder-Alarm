## **Privacy Policy**

AlphaMu Studios is hereby referred to as the "Service Provider".





### **Information Collection and Use**



The Application does not directly collect personal data. However, information may be

collected automatically through third-party services when you download and use the

Application.



* Your device's Internet Protocol address
* Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
* The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit,

the time spent on those pages

* Application crash logs, non-fatal error messages and performance analytics

The Application does not directly collect personally identifiable information such

as your name, email address, or phone number. However, it processes device-related

identifiers and usage data through third-party services for analytics, advertising,

and crash reporting purposes.





### **Device Permissions Required**

To function properly as a reliable alarm clock, the Application requires the following

system permissions. The Service Provider only uses these permissions to provide the

core functionality of the app, and no personal data from them is ever collected, stored,

or transmitted:



* Exact Alarm (SCHEDULE\_EXACT\_ALARM): Required to trigger your alarms precisely at the

exact second you have set them.



* Notification Permission: Required to display the active alarm status in your status bar

and to trigger alerts.



* Battery Optimization Bypass (REQUEST\_IGNORE\_BATTERY\_OPTIMIZATIONS): Required to prevent

the Android OS from putting the app to sleep, ensuring that your alarms will ring reliably

even if the device is in deep sleep.



* System Alert Window / Draw Over Other Apps: Required to display the full-screen alarm

dismiss interface over your lock screen or other running Applications when an alarm goes off.
