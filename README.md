# Wonder Alarm

Welcome to **Wonder Alarm**! 
Wonder Alarm is not just another simple alarm clock. It was designed for those who sleep heavily,
but also for those who love aesthetics and smart functionality in their daily lives.

Our goal is to ensure that you will never miss another important wake-up, while protecting your
peace of mind during the day.

## What makes Wonder Alarm unique:

* ** Retro VFD Widget:** A beautiful, nostalgic dot-matrix screen on your home screen that changes
colors (from "VFD Green" to "Imperial Red") to perfectly match your style.
* ** Absolute Awakening:** Forget accidental "Snooze" pressing in your sleep. To turn off the alarm,
your brain must be activated by solving clever math or memory puzzles.
* ** Dynamic Backgrounds:** The wake-up screen comes alive with alternating images and colors,
providing the necessary visual stimulus to open your eyes more easily and pleasantly.
* ** Max Volume Bypass:** If Android decides to lower the system volume, Wonder Alarm restores it
to 100% at the right time, to make sure you hear it.
* ** Mute on call:** If the alarm rings while you are in a call, don't worry. We have foreseen it.
If the phone is to your ear, you will hear a discreet beep from the earpiece and feel a light vibration.
If you have it on speakerphone, it will be heard at only 10% volume. Also, you won't see the classic
full dismiss screen, but a small popup that allows you to stop the alarm. *(Some models may not
support this feature.)*

##  Design, Themes & Ultimate Customization

We believe that an application you see right when you open your eyes should be beautiful, modern,
and tailored to you. That's why we gave huge emphasis to details:

* ** 3D Time Picker:** Forget boring, flat lists for selecting the time. Wonder Alarm features an
innovative, three-dimensional time selector that makes setting the alarm an enjoyable, smooth, and
fully interactive experience.
* ** Rich Color Palette (Themes):** Whether you are a lover of relaxing, pastel shades or you like
intense, vivid colors, there is a theme for you. Dress the app and your widget with unique premium
colors, such as elegant "Tiffany Blue", dynamic "Lava Red", and many other options that completely
renew the UI.
* ** Full International Support (9 Languages):** Wonder Alarm speaks your language! The app is fully
translated and adapted to 9 of the most popular languages in the world: *Greek, English, French,
German, Italian, Portuguese, Spanish, Russian, and Chinese* ensuring that every menu, instruction,
or puzzle is fully understandable wherever you are.
